7. Data Retention

We do not keep personal data longer than necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or longer than required by law.

In general:

  • Client and project-related data is kept for five (5) years after the end of the client relationship, unless a longer period is required by Dutch or EU law (for example for tax or accounting purposes) or agreed in a client contract.
  • Supplier and contract data is kept for five (5) years after the end of the contract, unless law requires a longer period.
  • Job applicant data is normally kept for up to one (1) year after the recruitment process, unless you consent to a longer retention for future opportunities or law requires a different period.
  • Employee data is retained in line with Dutch employment and tax rules, which may require retention for several years after the end of employment.
  • Client-provided datasets processed by us as a processor are retained only for the duration specified in the relevant contract or instructions, after which they are deleted or returned, unless we are legally obliged to retain them for longer.

When data is no longer needed, we delete it or irreversibly anonymise it.
